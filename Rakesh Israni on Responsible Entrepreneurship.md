# Rakesh Israni on Responsible Entrepreneurship 

More to business than just bottom line: Growing a profitable business isn’t everything. For Rakesh Israni, being a responsible entrepreneur involves building value, but maintaining integrity and focusing on a long term perspective. The consequences of business decisions always exist, and taking the right approach earns trust in the long run.

For many years, in his career, [Rakesh Israni](https://about.me/rakeshisranipacifica) always held that nothing can substitute consistent effort. No shortcuts. That solid relationships will come from dependable actions and honest communication with clients, partners and his team. These values held for him and still do.

Also include flexibility in responding to external events and changing markets, without being led too far from its roots. Being adaptable and fluid without drifting from its basic principles is crucial to surviving and thriving in the business world.

Leaders will always take the lead of those they work with, too, of course. Leadership is also all about nurturing the individuals that help facilitate the growth. We achieve this through a variety of strategies; this can include team building, valuing other people’s viewpoints, or providing opportunities to help individuals develop and improve. You might also find that it becomes common for motivation amongst your team members.

[Rakesh Israni](https://www.youtube.com/@RakeshIsraniPacifica) finds entrepreneurship an evolving journey where each challenge becomes an experience, and each success opens up new avenues for self improvement. Having a growth mindset gives individuals and organizations the confidence to make strides ahead.

Looking ahead, sound leadership will continue to play a role in making the most prosperous organizations. When a business emphasizes solid values, worthwhile relationships and consistent advancement, the result is a respected organization that also delivers on performance. That is what defines Rakesh Israni’s approach to business.
