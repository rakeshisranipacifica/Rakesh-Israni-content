# Rakesh Israni – Biography and Professional Information

[Rakesh Israni](https://dribbble.com/rakeshisranipacifica), more commonly known as "Rocky" Israni, is an Indian businessman and entrepreneur renowned for his deep involvement in business management and real estate development. As the Managing Director for Pacifica Companies India, he has played a pivotal role in driving the company's national expansion.

Rakesh israni is a Business Administration and Management graduate from Gujarat University and has worked with a vision for growth, innovation and the creation of long-term value in the sector. Associated with Pacifica since its inception, he was part of its team for the initial setup and growth of operations in India from 2005 onwards and has played an instrumental role in developing residential, commercial, retail and hospitality projects across cities.

In addition to working for Pacifica, Rakesh is Co-Founder of Frendy, which was established in 2019 and is a community-commerce platform aiding local entrepreneurs to sell products on social platforms—predominantly women entrepreneurs. He is the Founder of Nebula Infraspace LLP and is a director in multiple businesses.

With a reputation for his expertise, leadership, and passion for success, [Rakesh Israni](https://x.com/RakeshIsraniP) continues to shape the real estate and business world while stimulating and encouraging entrepreneurs and innovators to think beyond their comfort zones.
