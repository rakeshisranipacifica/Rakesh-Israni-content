# How Rakesh Israni Demonstrates the Importance of Practical Experience 

There’s a saying that we don’t grow until we work. “Knowledge and education give a person a good start, but it is in solving real-world problems that a person will acquire skills and wisdom that cannot be taught in a classroom or lecture hall, and grow to be more confident and have better judgement,” [Rakesh Israni](https://www.instagram.com/rakeshisrani/) states.

He has believed in learning on the go throughout his career. Each new assignment carries a different set of challenges and each new challenge means a new chance to learn and get ahead in life. It is his firmly held belief that theory is good for groundwork, but practical experience is essential to help you be resourceful enough to handle any adverse situation.

Also, you get to learn what books cannot. Working in high-pressure situations, interacting with the client and team members and overcoming unexpected hurdles, all these allow you to bloom into a responsible professional. [Rakesh Israni](https://isranirakesh.wordpress.com/2026/07/09/rakesh-israni-the-importance-of-adaptability-in-business/) emphasizes that facing these real-world scenarios firsthand is what truly shapes an individual into a highly capable and adaptable worker. 

Yet another element of pragmatic learning is a process of refinement. Through actual work, professionals tend to ascertain where they stand out and also where their talents may be honed and they could even go on to make smarter, more deliberate career choices. Rather than chasing rapid, short-term achievements, Israni strongly advocates for consistent progress over the mere pursuit of success. 

Importance of Practical Experience As people develop in their careers, practical experience will be very clear. It builds the confidence and credibility that make an individual dependable in the eyes of peers and clients alike.By adopting [rakesh Israni’s](https://www.instagram.com/rakesh_.israni/) mindset, professionals can build the momentum needed to achieve lasting professional success.
