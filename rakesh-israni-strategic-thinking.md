# Rakesh Israni: Creating Value Through Experience and Strategic Thinking

Experience and learning create much of our professional progress, making sure that we act with well-thought-out decisions. [Rakesh Israni](https://medium.com/@rakeshisranipacifica/rakesh-israni-a-positive-business-profile-1455bfd6d86a) embodies a style that’s as linked with practice as it is with foresight, and strategic planning. Within a constantly evolving climate, success involves not only responding but also anticipating future developments. 

However, perspective development depends on exposure too. Daily experiences expose individuals to different situations through a variety of challenges, successful feats, and common tasks. Over time, all the knowledge accumulated through experience helps you make choices wisely and get a grip on how to achieve better goals. Learning from the experiences of others can also help develop valuable ideas, as well.

Strategic thinking is also an element that cultivates a long-term perspective of life rather than being short-sighted in our dealings. A well-conceived approach to our careers and life can bring consistent progress through steady learning and adjustments. Strategic thinkers are often prepared to manage change well and identify potential opportunities.

Real value doesn't have to come from something huge and revolutionary. Often it's a result of many small steps, hard work, and learning along the way. Having the experience behind the choices and planning for the future is still a solid strategy for development.
