# Rakesh Israni: Managing Director of Pacifica and Co-Founder of Frendy

Business entrepreneur, [Mr. Rakesh Israni](https://medium.com/@rakeshisranipacifica) (Mr. Rocky Israni), is a man who has been a part of Pacifica's growth in India for many years now. Having been the Managing Director of Pacifica, he has contributed to the development of residential, commercial, retail and hospitality properties.

Rakesh Israni has played a key role in expanding Pacifica's footprint in the Indian real estate market. He has overseen development of projects with an aim to cater to evolving customer requirements and developing urban needs.

In addition to working with Pacifica, he is also recognized as the Co-Founder of Frendy, a community commerce platform that was launched in 2019. The intention behind Frendy was to help create community bonds and to offer individuals business participation on the social web.

[Rakesh Israni's](https://issuu.com/rakesh_israni_pacifica1) career provides an example of his experience in both real estate and business sectors. This reflects his drive to bring creativity to the business world while making a positive impact on society.
